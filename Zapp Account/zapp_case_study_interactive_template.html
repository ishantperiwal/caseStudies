Building Zapp Account

Rebrand and upgrade the existing digital wallet for PayZapp to Zapp Account, introducing Video KYC and creating entry points that could promote it across the app.

What is Zapp Account?

Zapp Account is a digital prepaid payments account offered by HDFC Bank through PayZapp, built to give users a more capable, controlled, and convenient way to pay, transact, and manage everyday money. It supports UPI, Tap and Pay, Peer to Peer, Peer to Merchant, Bank Transfers and Online Payments using a Virtual Card.

What we had to do

Introduce Video KYC to unlock full capabilities, and rebrand PayZapp Wallet to Zapp Account.

01 / Introduce Video KYC

The most immediate ask: until Video KYC, non-bank customers could not access the full capabilities of Zapp Account.

02 / Rebrand PayZapp Wallet

Comprehensive rebranding of all icons, nomenclature, and user nudges across the app and third-party merchant domains.

What we wanted to achieve

Increase wallet loads, improve adoption, and elevate the overall experience.

01 / Improve Adoption

Getting more non-bank users to enroll for Zapp Account via Video KYC, with a clear value proposition for a wider audience.

02 / Increase Wallet Loads

Auto Load was the clearest way to make Zapp Account habit-forming — users would not have to think about topping up each time.

03 / Elevate UX

Refresh the visual language, make the landing page more load-centric, and improve feature communication through nudges.

How did we approach it?

Using insights to derive opportunities, communicate core value, and organize the experience around contextual levers.

01 / Communicate Core Value Props

Speed, Security, and Convenience — leveraged Zapp Account capabilities to reinforce these across entry points and onboarding surfaces.

02 / Make Video KYC Clear and Trackable

Value-first carousel, clear expectations, progress states — so users understood where they were and what was left.

03 / Make the Home Page Load-Centric

Balance as the hero account state, Load Money as the hero action. Positioned Auto-Load near Load Money for discovery.

04 / Organize Around Contextual Levers

Core anchors, badges, and nudges that changed dynamically based on the user’s lifecycle with Zapp Account.

How did we solve it?

Following a non-bank user’s path from entry points through Video KYC to a fully unlocked account.

01 / Entry Points

Spotlight banners, MoEngage bottom sheets, and inline anchor icons across PayZapp surfaces introduced Zapp Account from familiar contexts.

02 / Video KYC Onboarding

Value-first carousel explained benefits → set clear expectations → trackable progress through each verification step.

03 / Unlocked Account

Full capabilities surfaced, load-centric homepage with Auto-Load, and contextual lifecycle nudges for ongoing education.

What were the results?

Double-digit month-on-month growth, top-5 ranking, and 1 in 5 loaders on Auto-Load.

What’s Next?

Future directions for Zapp Account and the broader PayZapp ecosystem.

01 / Expand & Scale

Expand Zapp Account to more PayZapp surfaces and merchant integrations, building on the entry-point strategy.

02 / Refine & Iterate

Continue refining the onboarding flow based on Video KYC completion data and lifecycle insights.

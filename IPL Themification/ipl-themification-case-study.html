for the IPL season

My RoleDesign Lead
DurationMar -- May 2025 (IPL Season)
FocusGrowth & Visual Design

The Brief

A visual overhaul of PayZapp to drive growth during the IPL season, partnering with Jio’s Dhan Dhana Dhan campaign alongside in-app initiatives focused on increasing activation and general engagement.

The Campaigns

Two growth initiatives designed to drive engagement, transactions, and new user acquisition during IPL.

Jeeto Dhan Dhana Dhan

While watching the IPL match answer cricket trivia questions and win coupons redeemable directly on PayZapp. This was partnered with Jio.

How to play

Score & Win

A campaign-specific score metric called 'Runs' powered a competitive leaderboard. Top scorers won prizes. Users earned runs by doing everyday PayZapp activities.

Design Tasks at Hand

Identify all the surfaces that could be revamped and themed within the existing constructs to minimize dev effort with a flavor of IPL and design artifacts to support the ongoing campaigns.

To bring the energy of the IPL season directly into the app, we set out to celebrate the tournament by overhauling key user-facing surfaces. Here is what we ended up designing and implementing to transform the in-app experience.
